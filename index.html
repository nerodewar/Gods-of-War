How to Play

RULES

Players are Orders seeking Elemental Powers. Explore the map, fight hordes, conquer dungeons, recruit Titans, call Gods, and dominate the realm.

Roll: Move across the map.

Dungeons: Defeat bosses to claim Elemental Powers.

Titans: Enhance your Powers.

Gods: Break the rules of war.
